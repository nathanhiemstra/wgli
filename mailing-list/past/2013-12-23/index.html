Featured Meeting

Friday 6:30pm - Antioch, IL

At the Antioch Recovery Club’s Friday night Adult Children of Alcoholics open meeting, newcomers are greeted with a handshake, an ACA Newcomers Pamphlet and an ACA welcome coin.

Our meetings focus heavily identifying with the Laundry List and on studying and analyzing the ACA Twelve Steps of Recovery. By practicing the steps and applying the principles to our daily lives, we learn to cope with the pain of growing up in a dysfunctional household, which enables us to begin to live our lives in a new way.  Each week, our group reads aloud from the ACA Red Book and other ACA related literature and members of the group discuss ways in which the selected material applies to their individual situation.

Though we often lack the sizable attendance numbers boasted by some groups in more populous areas, our group offers a very intimate, relaxed atmosphere in which each attendee is given ample time to share their story. We stress the importance of placing one’s trust in a higher power, and taking the steps one at a time. In addition, our group is very diverse and sports members of all ages and from all walks of life.

So if you’ve been searching for a friendly, accessible open ACA meeting in the Great Lakes area, stop by and see us Friday night at 6:30 PM!  For more information contact Dawn J. at 847-867-7592 or for other meetings and directions visit: http://www.antiochrecoveryclub.org/
