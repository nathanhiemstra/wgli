Featured Meeting

Sunday 5:30pm Red Text Meeting
Stillwaters Recovery Club, Lake Barrington, IL

This meeting reads the Red Text because we decided many of us were not reading it thoroughly enough on our own.  And even those who have read it over and over, love reading it again, as each time we read a section, we seem to be in a different place emotionally and hear something new.

Our format: On the first Sunday of the month we read the corresponding Step from the Red Text.  On the last Sunday we read the corresponding Tradition from the Red Text because they are what provide unity for ACA - and there's also information we can relate to personally.  On other nights, we read the rest of Red Text: we started at the very beginning and will read it through to the end.  We read for 10 minutes and then have timed shares so that everyone has an equal opportunity to be heard, something many of us didn't experience in our families of origin.  We share either about the reading or whatever else we're working on or dealing with. 

Our meeting usually has 7-12 participants with a consistent group of core attendees.  And often we go out for Fellowship after the meeting.

For more information, contact Mary Jo at 773-807-2986 or maryjl@msn.com.

The 2014 Annual Business Conference will be held in Chicago

What's happening: Our West Great Lakes ACA Intergroup is privileged to host the World Service Organization's (WSO) Annual Business Conference (ABC) next year. This is the same annual meeting that was held in Denmark this past April. We are excited by the opportunity to host people from around the country and the world at this event.

Who can attend: Any ACA. However, voting privileges are limited to one delegate per registered meeting. Other ACAs are welcome as observers.

More information: Official registration information will be sent from ACA WSO after the first of the year. Requests for ballot items should be sent soon, so your meeting's WSO registered point of contact will receive that.

How Can You help: Volunteers are needed to help with logistics, printing, assembly of delegate packets, catering ideas, fellowship activities and more. Please contact Al E. if you or a member of your meeting is interested in helping. Most of the work will not have to be done until the weeks leading up to the event next April.
